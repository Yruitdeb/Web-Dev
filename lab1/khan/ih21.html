Fukashigi no Kartl

Fukashigi no Karte is the ending songof the popular 2018 anime “Seishun Buta Yarō” or Rascal Does Not Dream of Bunny Girl Senpai. The song is sung by the six main seiyuus (voice actors) of the six main female characters.
